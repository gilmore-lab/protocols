3 Brain and Behavioral Dynamics Lab

3.1 Meetings

We will meet on Thursday mornings from 8-8:50am in person in room 350 Moore Building

3.2 Expectations

  • Attend and Participate in all meetings
  • If a meeting will be missed, notify the lab manager PRIOR to missing the meeting by Discord or email
  • Check out the meeting minutes on the Lab Website to see what was missed.
  • Come prepared for the meeting by completing any homework assignments.

3.3 Required Training

  • CITI Training - every 3 years
  • Learning Resource Network - yearly
    • 2022 Annual Compliance Training
    • Understanding Title IX at Penn State
    • Building a Safe Penn State: Reporting Child Abuse
    • Clery Act Training
  • Background Check (only if collecting data on minors)

3.4 Accounts needed

Account creation requests will be sent out with instructions via Asana.

  • Use Asana for task assignment
  • Use Discord for team communication
  • Use Git Hub for collaboration and version control
  • Use Datacamp to learn R
  • Use R Studio on the TLT Server

3.5 Frequently Used Resources
